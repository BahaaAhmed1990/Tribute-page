king of hearts

The time line of Dr .yacoub's life

  • 1935 - was born in Belbeis, Sharkia Governorate, Egypt.
  • 1957 - graduated from cairo university Faculty of medicine.
  • 1962 - moved to london work as doctor in Chest Hospital.
  • 1974 - first doctor to perform open heart surgery in Nigeria.
  • 1980 - Dr. Yaqoub heads the Heart Transplant Program at Harfield Hospital in London.
  • 1986 - He was appointed Professor at the National Heart and Lung Institute at the prestigious Imperial College London.
  • 1991 - The Queen of Britain awarded him a Sir title.
  • 1995 - He founded the "Chain of Hope" charitable organization which take care of children in conflict areas in developing countries who suffer from heart diseases.
  • 1999 - British Minister of Health awarded him Distinguished Performance Award for his contribution in the field of medicine.
  • 2009 - opened aswan heart center for heart in egypt.
